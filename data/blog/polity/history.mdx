# History

`january 26, 1959` the Constitution came into existance.

    1. **`The Company Rule (1773 - 1858)`**

# **The Company Rule (1773 - 1858)**
    1. ## `Regulating Act of 1773`
        1. ### **Importance**
            1. to regulate the affairs of the EIC
            1. first time, political and administration function of company
            1. central administration in India        
        1. ### **Features**
            1. Governor of Bengal -> `Governor-General of Bengal` (**`Lord Warren Hastings`**) & Executive council of 4 members to assist him
            1. Governor of Bombay and Madras - subordinate to the governor-general of Bengal
            1. estabishment of Supreme Court at Culcutta (1774)
            1. prohibated - any private trade or accepting any bribes 
            1. Court of Directors (governing body of Company)
